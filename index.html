                Usage:

                    
after install, OF command will add to your terminal 

                    
you can use it with 10 argument or use it single:

                    

                        
of -n OF will not organize file with no Sorting Folder

                        
of -h show help page

                        
of -d Oraganizing another directory instead of current directory

                        
of -v OF will say everything

                        
of -q OF will don't say anything

                        
of -f See all supported extension and their types

                        
of -s Search in supported formats

                        
of -a Add a new type for extension

                        
of -r reset all the custome types and extentions

                        
of -e OF will organize files by extension instead of their types!
